Trust & Security

How Clarynt handles data, security, and compliance so your team can move fast with confidence.

Data Handling

We prefer redacted or synthetic inputs for pilots. If you choose to share real data, limit to the minimum necessary.

  • No persistent PII by default; opt‑in only.
  • Temporary processing; deletion on request.
  • EU‑friendly processing on request.

Security

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest where stored.
  • Principle of least privilege; access logging.
  • Secrets stored as environment variables; rotated on change.
