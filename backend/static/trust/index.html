Clarynt Trust & Security Last updated: 2025-08-22

How we handle data, security, and compliance for your EU‑AI‑Act journey.

Overview

Clarynt helps teams prepare evidence and documentation for the EU AI Act. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice. We focus on secure handling of data and practical controls that reduce risk and speed up reviews.

Data Handling

  • Minimize first: We prefer redacted or synthetic inputs for pilots; no production access required.
  • Scoped access: Read‑only where possible; least‑privilege service accounts.
  • Export control: You approve the specific files we process. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Security Controls

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (provider‑managed KMS).
  • Access via SSO for internal accounts; MFA enforced.
  • Audit logging on administrative actions and data exports.

Sub‑processors

Sub‑processors are service providers we use to operate Clarynt. We will update this list prior to changes.

  • Render – hosting & runtime (data center region per your service configuration).
  • PostgreSQL (managed) – metadata storage (no raw customer content unless required by you).
  • OpenAI/LLM provider (optional) – used only when enabled for specific features; otherwise disabled in pilot (DEMO_MODE=1 meant calls were off).

Data Location & Retention

  • Location: Customer content is processed in the region configured for your service where possible.
  • Retention: Pilot artifacts retained for 90 days unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Deletion: Email security@clarynt.net to request deletion. We confirm within 5 business days.

Incidents & Vulnerabilities

  • Report security issues to security@clarynt.net. We acknowledge within 48 hours.
  • We patch critical dependencies promptly and review high‑risk alerts within 24 hours.

Compliance Notes

  • Clarynt helps generate documentation and evidence aligned to the EU AI Act and GDPR; your counsel decides final compliance posture.
  • DPA available on request. We support customer NDAs for pilots.
